Your Todo List v4.6

Track your todos

1-ADD
Add your todo by pressing the enter key or tap the '+' button.
2-SEE
You'll see it right away after you add a todo. Your latest todo will appear 1st on the list.
3-CHECK
Check off what you've done with the dark blue checkbox next to your todo. Mark all your todos as done with the dark blue checkmark button.
4-CHANGE
Make changes by tapping on your todo. Save it by pressing enter key on your keyboard or tap outside of the box. Cancel your change by pressing escape key on your keyword.
5-DELETE
Delete your todo by tapping the 'x' button next to your todo.
